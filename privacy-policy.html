1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this mobile application collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the app and related services. It is written to support transparency for Apple App Store and Google Play requirements.

The app provides AI-powered photo restoration features such as colourisation, enhancement, scratch removal, and face restoration.

2. Information We Collect

Analytics and usage information

We collect limited analytics and technical information so the app can function properly, improve product quality, understand feature usage, and monitor stability.

  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, and general diagnostic details.
  • Feature interaction events, session activity, performance data, and crash information.
  • General product analytics collected through PostHog and Google Analytics.

Backend and account-related information

We use Firebase to support backend features such as anonymous user identification, coin balance management, purchase-related state, rate limiting, and usage tracking.

  • Anonymous user or installation identifiers.
  • App usage metadata needed to operate the coin system and prevent abuse.
  • Technical records related to service delivery and security.

Image data

When you choose to process a photo, the image is sent to third-party AI processing services through our backend or service flow so the requested transformation can be completed.

  • Original image submitted by you for processing.
  • Temporary processing inputs and generated outputs needed to complete the request.
We do not state that no third party ever temporarily handles image data. Your image is necessarily transmitted for processing when you use an AI feature.

3. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide AI image processing and deliver generated results.
  • To maintain your app experience, including coins, subscriptions, purchases, and feature access.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, usability, and product decisions.
  • To detect abuse, fraud, suspicious activity, or misuse of free credits and paid credits.
  • To troubleshoot bugs, crashes, latency issues, and service failures.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Third-Party Services

We use selected third-party service providers to support app functionality.

  • Firebase for backend infrastructure, authentication-related services, database, and service operations.
  • Replicate for AI image processing.
  • PostHog for product analytics and event insights.
  • Google Analytics for analytics and app usage measurement.

These providers may process information on our behalf according to their own terms and privacy documentation.

5. Image Handling and Storage

Your images are processed only for the purpose of completing the feature you requested.

  • We do not intend to permanently store your images on our own servers as part of normal app operation.
  • Images may be temporarily processed by third-party services needed to generate the requested output.
  • Generated images may be returned to your device, and you are responsible for saving or managing copies stored locally on your device.
This wording is safer for store review than claiming absolute zero storage everywhere, because temporary processing by service providers may occur.

6. Legal Bases and User Controls

Depending on your location, we may process data based on one or more of the following: your use of the service, our legitimate interests in operating and improving the app, legal compliance needs, and where required, your consent.

You may also limit analytics or advertising-related controls through your device settings where those options are available.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only where needed to operate the app, deliver requested features, process purchases, support analytics, protect the service, or comply with law.

  • Service providers that support hosting, analytics, infrastructure, and AI processing.
  • Authorities, regulators, or law enforcement when legally required.
  • Successors in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to applicable law.

8. Data Retention

We retain different categories of information for different periods depending on operational need, legal requirements, fraud prevention, dispute handling, and technical necessity.

  • Analytics and diagnostic data may be retained for product improvement and security analysis.
  • Backend records may be retained while needed to support the service, purchases, abuse prevention, and lawful business operations.
  • We do not intend to keep uploaded images permanently on our own servers as part of normal operation, although temporary processing retention by service providers may occur.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures designed to protect information. However, no system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information in violation of this policy, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

11. International Transfers

Because our service providers may operate in multiple countries, your information may be processed outside your country or region. By using the app, you understand that information may be transferred to locations with different data protection laws.

12. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability for certain personal information. You may also request information about how your data is handled.

To make a request, contact help@bluepointer.in. We may need to verify your request before responding.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date. Continued use of the app after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

14. Contact Us

For privacy questions, support requests, or data-related requests, contact help@bluepointer.in.
