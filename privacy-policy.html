Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Analytics Data

We collect limited usage data to improve the app, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Crash reports

This data is collected using PostHog and Google Analytics.

b) Firebase Data

We use Firebase to manage backend services, including:

  • Anonymous user identification
  • Coin balance and usage tracking

c) Image Data

Images uploaded in the app are:

  • Sent securely to third-party AI services (Replicate API)
  • Processed temporarily
  • Not stored permanently on our servers

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality
  • To monitor performance and fix bugs
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Replicate (AI image processing)
  • Firebase (backend infrastructure)
  • PostHog (analytics)
  • Google Analytics (analytics)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data is shared only with the services listed above to operate the app.

5. Data Retention

  • Images: Not stored
  • Analytics data: Retained for performance improvement
  • Firebase data: Stored while your account is active

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Deletion of your data

8. Children's Privacy

9. International Users

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.
